What is Pylon Signage?

Pylon signage refers to freestanding vertical signs that are typically mounted on poles or columns and placed outside commercial properties, along highways, at business entrances, or near traffic junctions. These signs are often large, elevated, and double-sided, making them visible from afar. Businesses prefer pylon signs for their impressive height, clear visibility, and unmatched presence.

These signs come in various styles including digital pylon signs, LED pylon signs, 3D pylon signage, and totem pylon signs—each designed to suit different business types and locations. Whether you run a gas station, shopping center, hospital, or restaurant, commercial pylon signs help make your location easy to spot and remember.

Why Pylon Signage Works for Businesses

Unlike typical storefront signage, pylon signs are elevated and placed at strategic entry points or roadside locations to attract the attention of passing vehicles and pedestrians. This makes them ideal for businesses set back from the road or operating within a commercial complex.

Here’s why pylon signs are a smart investment:

  • High Visibility – Easily seen from a distance, especially important for high-traffic areas.

  • Professional Image – Reflects brand authority and professionalism.

  • Customizable – From small pylon signs to multi-tenant pylon signs, designs can be tailored.

  • Durable & Weatherproof – Built to withstand harsh outdoor conditions.

  • Night-time Impact – With LED pylon signs, your brand shines even after dark.

Types of Pylon Signage We Offer

1. Single-Tenant Pylon Signs

Ideal for standalone businesses like showrooms, offices, or restaurants. These are usually tall, single-branded structures with high visibility.

2. Multi-Tenant Pylon Signs

Commonly used in shopping complexes, commercial buildings, or industrial parks. These signs feature multiple business names stacked vertically, making them perfect for locations with multiple tenants.

3. Digital Pylon Signs

Combining modern technology with traditional structure, these signs feature LED displays or digital screens to display dynamic content such as offers, updates, or promotions.

4. 3D Pylon Signage

For a premium, stylish look that makes your brand pop, 3D pylon signage adds depth, lighting effects, and creative styling.

5. Totem Pylon Signs

Slim and vertical, totem signs are sleek, modern, and ideal for minimalistic branding approaches. These are commonly seen in corporate business parks and luxury retail zones.

6. Gas Station Pylon Signs

Highly durable and compliant with fuel station branding guidelines, these signs are designed for visibility on highways and arterial roads.

7. Pylon Monument Signs

Closer to ground level, these are perfect for businesses looking for elegant, architectural signage with wide bases and premium finishes.
