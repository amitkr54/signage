Signage Company in Gurgaon

Gurgaon has changed dramatically over the last two decades — and so has its demand for quality signage. When we started in 2004, most of the large corporate campuses in Cyber City and DLF were still being built. Today, we regularly supply signage to the offices, malls, hospitals, restaurants, and residential projects that define this city.

We are based in Lado Sarai, South Delhi, which puts us about 20-25 minutes from most of Gurgaon. Our team does site surveys, fabrication, and installation for all Gurgaon projects without any extra logistics cost in most cases.

Why Gurgaon Businesses Come to Us

Gurgaon has a high concentration of corporate offices, tech parks, and retail spaces — and all of them need professional signage. The standard expected here is higher than most other markets. Clients want clean fabrication, precise installation, and signs that don't fade or flicker after a few months. That's what we focus on.

We have done office signage systems for companies in Cyber City towers, retail fit-outs in malls on MG Road and Sohna Road, restaurant neon signs in DLF Cyberhub, and mandatory safety signage for factories in Manesar and IMT.

Corporate and Office Signage in Gurgaon

Gurgaon's corporate offices typically need a complete signage package: reception signage, floor directory boards, meeting room name plates, cabin nameplates, motivational wall graphics, and sometimes large facade LED letters for the building exterior. We handle all of this as a single project with one point of contact.

For larger office complexes, we also provide wayfinding and directional signage systems - helping visitors and employees navigate multi-floor and multi-wing buildings efficiently. This is particularly common in IT parks and hospital campuses around Gurgaon.

Restaurants and Retail in Gurgaon

The F&B and retail sector in Gurgaon is growing fast. Cyberhub, Sector 29, MG Road malls, and Sohna Road high-streets all have businesses that need standout signage. We have made neon signs, backlit menu boards, shop fascia LED boards, and outdoor flex banners for restaurants and stores across these areas.

Neon signs for restaurants - the LED neon flex type on transparent acrylic or wood backing - are something we make regularly for Gurgaon clients. They photograph well, look great in the evening, and last for years with minimal maintenance.
