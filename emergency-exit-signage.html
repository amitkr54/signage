Importance of Emergency Exit Signage
Emergency situations can occur without warning. In such moments, visibility is everything. Our emergency exit signs are designed to offer clear, illuminated pathways to exits, even in low light or smoke-filled environments. Using high-contrast graphics, directional arrows, and universal symbols, these signs provide guidance even to non-English speakers.

We provide:

  • Photoluminescent (glow-in-the-dark) exit signage

  • LED illuminated exit signs

  • Directional emergency signage

  • Double-sided and ceiling-mounted emergency signage

  • Custom emergency exit boards with your logo or building layout

Our signs meet ISO, OSHA, and BIS compliance standards, ensuring your building meets both local and international regulations.

Types of Emergency Exit Signage We Offer

  • Wall-Mounted Emergency Exit Boards

  • Ceiling-Hanging Exit Signage

  • Directional Exit Signs with Arrows

  • Emergency Staircase and Corridor Signs

  • Running Man Exit Signs

  • Bilingual Exit Signs (English + Hindi)

  • Custom Emergency Evacuation Plans with Signage

  • Fire Exit Route Boards

Crafted using durable materials like aluminum composite panels (ACP), acrylic, vinyl, and polycarbonate, our signs are waterproof, UV-resistant, and built to withstand harsh conditions both indoors and outdoors.
