Danger Signage: Critical for Safety and Compliance
Danger Signage plays a crucial role in maintaining safety across construction sites, industrial facilities, roads, and commercial properties. These signs act as a visual warning system that informs, alerts, and protects workers, visitors, and the general public from potential hazards. At Sign & Display Solutions, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality, durable danger signs that comply with industry standards and regulatory requirements.

Whether it’s a construction site danger sign, hazard warning signage, or electrical danger sign, each is designed with clear visibility and bold messaging to grab attention immediately. Our signs are available in reflective vinyl, aluminum, ACP, or acrylic materials for both indoor and outdoor applications.

 

Why Danger Signage Matters
Danger signs are not just informative—they are legally required on high-risk premises. They protect lives by clearly communicating hazards such as electrical risks, chemical exposure, construction zones, or restricted areas. Our custom danger signs come in standard red-black-white formats, complying with OSHA and ISO signage regulations.

From “Danger: Construction Area Keep Out” signs to “Danger: High Voltage”, our collection includes both pre-designed and customizable danger signage solutions tailored to your site needs. We also offer multilingual danger signs for bilingual sites across Delhi NCR, Gurgaon, and Noida.

Materials & Mounting Options
Our danger signage is crafted using durable, weather-resistant materials such as ACP, aluminum, sunboard, and reflective films. Choose from:

  • Wall-mounted danger boards

  • Pole-mounted warning signs

  • Floor stand danger signs

  • Glow-in-the-dark danger signage

  • 3D raised text signs for industrial areas

Where to Use Danger Signage

  • Construction Sites: “Danger – Men at Work”, “Danger – Excavation Area”

  • Electrical Rooms: “Danger – High Voltage”, “Authorized Personnel Only”

  • Factories and Warehouses: “Danger – Moving Machinery”, “Chemical Storage Area”

  • Road Safety Zones: “Danger – Road Work Ahead”, “Danger – Uneven Surface”

  • Commercial Buildings: Fire exits, machine rooms, restricted zones
